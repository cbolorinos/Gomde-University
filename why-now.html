Become a Founder

The defining problem
of our time

The world has mastered the accumulation of knowledge. It has not mastered the wisdom to apply it.

The gap between the two is now the defining problem of our time. The Kremsegg University exists to close it: a degree-granting institution that forms the whole person, holds contemplative wisdom in rigorous dialogue with modern science, and prepares people to lead with judgment, compassion, and clarity in a world that urgently needs them.

The demand is real

This is not a hope in search of an audience. In research across three continents — with prospective students, long-term practitioners, and accomplished professionals of no Buddhist background — people from diverse and often opposite backgrounds, who shared almost nothing, arrived at the same conviction: existing institutions teach knowledge and skill but leave the person unchanged, and something different is needed now. The demand is real, the moment is open, and the foundations are in place.

What remains is to build it

A founding gift does not support an established institution — it brings one into being. The decisions made in these first years will shape this university for the century its founders intend it to serve. There will only ever be one founding, and the people who make it possible will be remembered as the ones who acted at the moment that mattered.
