Become a Founder

Every founding gift
builds something specific

There are several ways to take part in the founding of the Kremsegg University.

  • Major gifts toward the property and the capital campaign — the investments that make the institution possible.
  • Endowed chairs and professorships to attract and hold world-class faculty.
  • Scholarship funds to ensure this education reaches students of every background.
  • Program support for specific academic or cultural initiatives.
  • Joining the Founders Circle, the community of visionary donors establishing this new paradigm in education.

Tax-deductible giving

The Kremsegg University is part of the Shedrub Mandala and benefits from an established network of charitable structures across multiple countries. Donors in the United States can give through the Shedrub Development Fund, a 501(c)(3) organization, making contributions fully tax-deductible. Tax-advantaged giving options exist in other jurisdictions as well.

We work with each donor to ensure a contribution is processed through the channel most beneficial to their situation — including gifts of cash, securities, multi-year pledges, and gifts from family foundations and donor-advised funds.

In Europe, tax treatment depends on your country of residence and your individual circumstances. We are glad to provide the information your advisors need, and we encourage donors to confirm the treatment of any gift with their own tax or financial advisor.
