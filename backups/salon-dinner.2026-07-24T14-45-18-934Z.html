It is our great pleasure to invite you

On the evening of 12 September, twenty guests will gather by invitation at a baroque castle in the heart of Upper Austria — a place that has held space for learning, music, and quiet revolutions of thought since the year 1230.

It will become the setting for a private benefit dedicated to a new kind of university: one that brings together the depth of the Buddhist contemplative tradition with the rigor of modern scholarship, with the aim of shaping future leaders guided by wisdom, clarity, compassion, and inner coherence.

Guests will be received at the castle gates and led into the Golden Salon of Luminosity, where the Austrian artist Alexandra-Maria Marcus has composed an immersive experience of light and sound — designed to stir the conversation and awaken the senses.

At the heart of the evening, one essential question

What does it mean to lead as a force for elevation in a world that is interconnected?
