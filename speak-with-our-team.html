Become a Founder

A conversation,
before anything else

A founding gift begins with a conversation, not a form.

If you feel drawn to this work, we would welcome the chance to understand what matters to you, to share the campaign in detail, and to explore how your support might take shape.

Conversations with prospective founding partners are private, unhurried, and held directly with the Founding Rector and the development team. There is no expectation attached to a first conversation — only the opportunity to learn more and to ask anything you wish.
