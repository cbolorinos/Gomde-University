Become a Founder

A relationship no later gift
can replicate

Those who make a significant investment at the founding level are recognized, in perpetuity, as Founding Patrons — the visionaries who helped bring this institution into being at the moment that mattered.

To be a Founding Patron is to hold a relationship with the University at Kremsegg that no later gift can replicate:

  • Recognition that endures. Your role in the founding is permanently part of the institution’s history and identity.
  • A direct relationship with the university’s leadership. Founding Patrons work closely with the Founding Rector and senior team — not at a distance, but as genuine partners in the early life of the institution.
  • A real and time-limited opportunity to shape direction. In the founding years, the institution’s priorities, programs, and character are still being formed. Founding Patrons have a singular opportunity to help shape them during the only window in which that shaping is possible.

Founding Patrons join the Founders Circle — a community of donors committed to establishing a new paradigm in higher education, convened for conversation, gatherings, and a shared stake in what this university becomes.
